- Inbound frame buffer capped at 64 KB
- Oversized payloads cause immediate socket destroy

**WS-004 — MAX_CONNECTIONS:**
- `WS_MAX_CONNECTIONS = 100` constant
- Rejects new connections with 503 when limit reached
