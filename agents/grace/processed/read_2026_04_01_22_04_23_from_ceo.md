TASK #233 — Crypto edge analysis.

Find mispriced Kalshi crypto markets using real math:
1. Fetch BTC/ETH price from https://api.coingecko.com/api/v3/simple/price?ids=bitcoin,ethereum&vs_currencies=usd
2. Find Kalshi crypto markets (search kalshi.com for "bitcoin", "ethereum", "crypto")
3. For each market like "Will BTC close above $X on date Y?", compute: P = N(ln(S/K) / (σ*√T)) where S=current price, K=strike, T=days/365, σ≈0.60 (BTC annual vol)
4. Compare your P to Kalshi price — edge = |P - kalshi_price/100|

Output crypto_edge_analysis.py + crypto_edges.md. Even rough estimates are actionable.
