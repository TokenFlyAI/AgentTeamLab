# Kalshi Market Research — Draft Notes

## Market Categories on Kalshi

Based on API docs and platform data, Kalshi organizes markets into:

1. **Economics & Finance** — Fed funds rate decisions, CPI (headline/core), nonfarm payrolls, unemployment rate, GDP growth, debt ceiling
2. **Politics & Elections** — Presidential elections, congressional races, primaries, legislation passage, approval ratings, cabinet picks
3. **Sports** — NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, college football/basketball, soccer, UFC, golf, tennis
4. **Crypto** — BTC/ETH price thresholds, ETF approvals, regulatory outcomes
5. **Entertainment & Culture** — Oscars, Grammys, box office, Rotten Tomatoes, Spotify charts, video game releases
6. **Weather & Science** — Named storms, temperature ranges, climate milestones, SpaceX launches

## Volume & Liquidity Profile

- **Total 2025 volume**: ~$43.1B (Kalshi)
- **Weekly volume (Feb 2026)**: ~$2.7B (53% market share vs Polymarket)
- **Active markets**: ~3,500
- **Sports dominance**: 90% of Kalshi volume during 2025-26 peak season (NFL/NBA/MLB)
- **Economics markets**: $5-20M open interest on top contracts, 0.5-1 cent spreads
- **Kalshi spreads**: 3-8 cents average; slippage 2-4% on 5,000-contract orders

## Key Research Findings

### Economics
- NBER/Fed study (Diercks, Katz, Wright): Kalshi mode has **perfect forecast record** on Fed funds rate decisions since 2022.
- Kalshi CPI forecasts: **40.1% lower MAE** than Bloomberg consensus overall; **50-60% lower MAE** during shock events.
- Divergence signal: when Kalshi diverges from consensus by >0.1pp, probability of shock = **81.2%**.
- Less documented: NFP and GDP forecast accuracy. These are potential edge areas.

### Politics
- 2024 election: Kalshi 48/50 states correct; Polymarket 49/50. Polls missed swing states by 3-5 points.
- Presidential markets are highly efficient. Down-ballot/primary/legislation markets have lower liquidity = more inefficiency.

### Sports
- Highest volume = deepest liquidity, but retail-heavy participation creates behavioral inefficiencies.
- Well-documented biases: favorite-longshot bias, recency bias, public-team overreaction.

### Crypto
- Growing segment but smaller than macro/politics/sports.
- Price-threshold markets are essentially binary options on extremely efficient spot markets.
- Edge is harder to find because information is instant and widely available.

## Preliminary Top 3 Edge Opportunities

1. **Economics: NFP & CPI Nowcasting** — High confidence. Alternative data + shock-alpha research support this.
2. **Politics: Down-ballot & Legislative Outcomes** — Medium-high confidence. Less liquid, polls are systematically biased.
3. **Sports: NFL/NBA Systematic Models** — Medium confidence. High volume, rich data, behavioral inefficiencies.

Crypto ranks below Sports for pure edge potential despite Founder's prioritization — will explain in final report.
