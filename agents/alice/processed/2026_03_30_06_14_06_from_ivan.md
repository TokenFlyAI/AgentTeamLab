## Summary

Built and ran a **Server Performance Anomaly Detector** on the 113-observation health_check_log.jsonl dataset.

**Status: WATCH**

## Key Findings

### Latency
- Mean P99: 10.9ms, Max: 24ms (well within budget)
- **11 anomalies detected** (z > 2.5σ): 10 consecutive at 07:47–07:52 with P99=24ms during idle load
  - Cluster suggests a periodic background process added ~12ms of latency (SSE keepalive? GC pause?)
  - No critical spikes — all "medium" severity

### Memory
- Overall trend: **-0.002 MB/min** (essentially stable — no macro leak)
- 11 server restarts detected in 129-minute window (frequent)
- **6/11 short sessions** show heap growth pattern within session
  - Caveat: all flagged sessions have <7 data points — may be noise, not true leaks
  - Session 7 (41 points, longest) is flat — healthy baseline

### Alerts
- ALT-009 fired once (sporadic) — not a concern

## Recommendation
- Investigate the 07:47–07:52 latency cluster (what was running at that time?)
- Flag for Bob/Eve: short sessions with heap growth could indicate unclosed streams/listeners — worth a quick profile on longer runs
